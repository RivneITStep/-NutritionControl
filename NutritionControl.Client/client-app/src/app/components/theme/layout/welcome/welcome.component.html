Understanding Macronutrients

The three macronutrients are carbohydrates (carbs), fats and protein. These nutrients are needed in relatively large amounts. They provide calories and have various functions in your body. All foods are grouped in tables. Each such table contains information on the amount of proteins, fats, carbohydrates and calories per 100 g. product.

Plan your meals by the week or even the month

One of the best ways to have a healthy diet is to prepare your own food and eat in regularly. Pick a few healthy recipes that you and your family like and build a meal schedule around them.

Why Portion Control is Important

Your calorie intake is a key factor in weight control and health. By controlling your portions, you are more likely to avoid consuming too many calories. While whole foods are certainly a lot harder to overeat than processed foods, they can still be eaten in excess. If you are overweight or trying to lose body fat, it's particularly important to monitor your portion size.
